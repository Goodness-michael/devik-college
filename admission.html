Admission

Welcome to all newly admitted students of Devik Vocational School.All admitted students are required to report to the admission office in the school's administration bloock within 14days of arrival.Admitted students should come with the following:

  • Offer Letter
  • Highest Diploma Certificate
  • Birth certificate

These mentioned documents will help us generate student i.d card which will be required for obtaining a temporary resident card. Students are required to take the student i.d card to the regional home office for application of temporary residence card.

Acommodation

There different accomodation option available for students.We have a hostel which is fully furnished with basic student amenities. students can choose from regular room, standard room and executive

Regular Room: This room consist of a room with 2 shared bed space, having shared amenities like toilet, kitchen, washing machine and television.This room cost £250 per student for a month.

Standard Room:This room consist of a single bed space for only one student.It has bathroom and kitchen ensuite.This room goes for £350 per month.

Executive Room:This room consist of a larger room space with television, washing machine ,Reading table, sofa and kitchen ensuite. This room is mostly kept for mothers studying with their child.This room cost £450 per month.

Note:All payments should be made with at least 3months deposit payment.
