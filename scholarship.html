SCHOLARSHIPS

Every year Devik vocational school are given some scholarship slots from both governmental and non-governmental organizations.These scholarships are given to students who meet the required criteria for the scholarship.Below are some of the scholarships available to our students.


Devik Vocational Scholarship.

This scholarship is given to students who:

  • Have obtained and maintained a high grade after their first year of study.Half of their tuition fee will be paid by the school,in addition to a monthly stipen for their monthly expenses
  • Students who demostrate they are from a refugee country,Asylum seekers and orphans.This scholarship is offered based on humanitarian grounds.IN this scholarship tuition fee is paid in full for the student with a monthly stipen
  • Students with disability.This is a full time scholarship

United Nation Women Scholarship

This scholarship is focused on women.This scholarship is designed for the purpose of women empowerment.Financial aids are given to women who develop strong passion to learning vocational skills.This schorlarships serves as a motivation to raise more skillful women in the society to encourage gender equality in vocational career field.To qualify for this scholarship a student should submit a study motivational letter explaining why she deserves the scholarship, dreams goals and aspiration after graduation.
